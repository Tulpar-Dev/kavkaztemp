    ABOUT US

    Reefer, DryVan or Stepdeck we got you covered. Being a truck driver for 5 years himself the founder of Kavkaz Express, LLC knows the business from inside out. With dedication, hard work and compassion he managed to build a strong and trustworthy name.

    Today Kavkaz Express LLC is recognized as a successful and fast-growing transportation company that serves customers within USA and Canada. Founded in 2012 with a single truck on the fleet, Kavkaz Express LLC has expanded and became one of the leaders in the industry operating with 3 locations and multiple warehouses.

     

    From day one Kavkaz Express LLC has prioritized the success of its partners and employees and therefore, has embraced one fundamental rule: treat others as you wish to be treated yourself. Core values and principles of our company are based on this foundation.
